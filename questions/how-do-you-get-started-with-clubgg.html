How do you get started with ClubGG?

clubgg Independent educational Q&A. Last updated 2026-07-09.

Short answer: To get started with ClubGG, install or open the app, understand how club IDs/referrals work, choose a club that matches your goals, review the rules, and start conservatively. The safest first step is education and comparison, not rushing into the first club link you see.

Basic steps

  • Install/open the app and create your account according to platform rules.
  • Use a trusted club guide or club ID to find the intended group.
  • Read onboarding instructions, rules, and support details.
  • Start small while you learn the interface and community norms.

What to avoid

  • Do not join groups based only on hype or vague screenshots.
  • Do not ignore local laws, platform rules, or responsible bankroll limits.
  • Do not treat any private club as guaranteed income.
