How do referral IDs and club IDs work?

glossary Independent educational Q&A. Last updated 2026-07-09.

Short answer: A club ID usually helps you find a specific club, while a referral ID or agent reference may identify who introduced or supports a player. The exact meaning can vary by app and community, so beginners should confirm instructions from a trusted guide before submitting any ID.

Simple distinction

  • Club ID: points to the club/community you want to join.
  • Referral ID: may connect your signup to an agent, guide, or support contact.
  • Both should be verified before use because typos or random IDs can route you to the wrong place.

Safe handling

  • Use IDs only from a source you trust.
  • Save screenshots or notes of the instructions you followed.
  • Ask support what each ID is used for before proceeding if unclear.
