How these reports should be cited

Use these reports when a page needs an objective framework rather than another generic paragraph. For example, a club ID article can cite the terminology map, a safety article can cite the scorecard, and a ClubGG vs PokerBros article can cite the comparison matrix. Keep links contextual and limited. The reports are meant to help readers evaluate details, not to create repetitive exact-match anchors.
