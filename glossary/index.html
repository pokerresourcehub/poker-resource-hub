Private Poker Club Glossary

Plain-English definitions for private poker club terms used in PokerBros, ClubGG, and beginner poker strategy research.

Club ID

A club ID is the code or identifier used to find a specific private poker club inside an app.

Referral ID

A referral ID usually identifies who referred or supports a player, while a club ID identifies the club itself.

Agent

An agent is commonly a support or referral contact for a private poker club; the role and responsibilities vary by club.

Union

A union is a network of clubs that may share traffic, games, and player pools. Bigger networks can offer more games, but still require safety checks.

Private Poker Club

A private poker club is a community-run club or group inside a poker app with its own rules, schedules, support, and joining process.

ClubGG Club

A ClubGG club is a private or semi-private community in the ClubGG ecosystem with its own club instructions and rules.

PokerBros Club

A PokerBros club is a private community inside the PokerBros app where players find games through club IDs, referrals, or guides.

Bankroll Limit

A bankroll limit is a personal cap that helps players avoid playing stakes that are too large for their budget or experience.

Hand History

A hand history is a record or replay of a poker hand used for review, learning, and strategy improvement.

HUD Stats

HUD stats are tracked poker statistics used by some players to review tendencies and make more informed decisions.
